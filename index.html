Biography

I am a Ph.D. researcher at Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU), supervised by Prof. Pong Chi Yuen, and currently a Visiting Scholar at Nanyang Technological University (NTU).


Standing at the forefront of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Autonomous Agents, my research is dedicated to revolutionizing Biopharmaceuticals. I aim to build the next generation of "AI Scientists"—intelligent agents capable of reasoning through complex biological mechanisms to accelerate De Novo drug design and protein engineering.


My work bridges the gap between generative AI and scientific reality. By integrating Geometric Deep Learning with Embodied Intelligence, I am developing closed-loop systems where AI agents not only design novel molecules but also perceive and interact with the physical world for verification. With a background as an AI Startup Founder, I possess the full-stack engineering capability to translate these visionary theories into robust, high-performance systems.
